As Lumbini lies in Terai region of Nepal, it is easily accessible from almost every places of Country


Kathmandu to Lumbini

Lumbini located at Tarai plains of southern Nepal. The sacred place where Lord Buddha was born in 623 BC. It is 272 kilometer from Kathmandu. There are 3 ways to reach Lumbini from Kathmandu. Besides visiting only Lumbini Garden other important sites to visit related Buddha are Kapilavastu, where Sidhartha Gautama spend his life up to 29 years before he becomes a Gautama Buddha. It is 25 kilometer south-west from Lumbini. World peace stupa situated a bit outside of Lumbini Garden, The shining golden statue depicts the Buddha in the posture he assumed when he was born.

Kathmandu Lumbini by bus Bus drive may take a long time about 9-10 hours. There is a tourist bus driving from Kathmandu to Lumbini. Local buses are also available Kathmandu to Bhairahawa. Travel Nepal tourist bus service has started their tourist bus service from Kathmandu. They leave every day from both Kathmandu and Lumbini. Bus fare USD 15-20. Depend on how much service charge put by a travel agency. A bus is a comfortable tourist bus with air-conditioning. They leave 7 am from Kathmandu.

Lumbini by Flight from Kathmandu There are about 10 flights every day from Kathmandu. It is to land at Bhairahawa airport and another 27-kilometer drive takes almost 1 hour to reach Lumbini. The flight fair for other than Indian and Nepalese is USD 116 per person per way. (December 2019).
